Where I Am From, Where I Have Been, And Where I Want To Go

I was born and grew up in the Metro Detroit area, spending most of my childhood in Redford, MI. I rented my first apartment in downtown Detroit from my late teens to early 20s. I moved into a condo unit at age 22 in downtown Dearborn where I fell in love with the community here. I purchased my first home in Dearborn Heights at 24 where I still live today, and love my little home more each day.

I love to travel out west. Arizona, Nevada, and Colorado have been my favorite places to visit. I plan to visit more of New Mexico and Oregon within the next few years. It is my dream to find a home in Nevada or Arizona to settle into a place with no snow.

Before my life comes to end, I want to travel to New Zealand. I plan to spend as much time hiking and Whale Watching from every shore.

My Development Journey

While I have worked in my current career for about 10 years, I have been searching for something more fulfilling. I have been looking for a career where I can express my creativity and problem solve. I discovered software development through my current employer and have been enthusiastic about it ever since. I am proud of the projects I have made using my new skills and am excited to see where these new skills take me.
